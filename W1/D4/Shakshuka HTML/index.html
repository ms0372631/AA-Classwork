Choco Coffee

  1. Material Prep
  2. Directions

This is what you need to prepare:

  1. Coffee beans
  2. Cream
  3. Chocolate

Directions:

  1. Put the beans in the coffee machine
  2. Throw chocolate inside the machine before turning on the machine
  3. Pour out the coffee and add cream on it
